Faber est suae, quisque fortunae.

Growing up in the midwest, I was taught to work hard, continually look for improvement, and to strive to make a difference.
This is how I live my life. By look for opportunities to grow both at work and at home, I have developed technological skills outside of my job requirements. I hope to instill this attitude to my daughter as she grows.

This is what fuels my need to acquire technological skills and be a part of that impact.
